Description

A cheesy bolognese lasagna recipe by @DAYINTHEKITCHEN.

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8 cups Beefy Tomato Bolognese Sauce
  • Gluten-free Lasagna noodles
  • 3-4 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200C)
  2. Layer in baking dish: Bolognese sauce, light sprinkle of cheese, gluten-free lasagna noodles. Repeat layers once more.
  3. Top with remaining Bolognese sauce, making sure to cover any exposed lasagna noodle edges. Cover with remaining cheese.
  4. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  5. Remove foil and bake for another 20 minutes or until the cheese is brown and bubbly.
  6. Let rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
